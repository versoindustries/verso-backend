# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Verso! This guide will help you get started with your new business operating system.

## 5-Minute Setup

### Step 1: Access Your Dashboard

1. Open your browser and go to your Verso URL
2. Click **Login** in the top right
3. Enter your credentials provided by your administrator
4. You'll arrive at your personal dashboard

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

1. Click your name in the top right corner
2. Select **My Profile**
3. Add your:
   - Profile photo
   - Contact information
   - Timezone preference
4. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Explore the Navigation

| Section | What You'll Find |
|---------|------------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your personal overview and activity |
| **Messages** | Internal team communication |
| **Calendar** | Your appointments and schedule |
| **Shop** | Browse products and services |
| **Blog** | Read company news and articles |

## Getting Help

### In-App Help

- Look for **?** icons for contextual help
- Hover over any field for tooltips
- Check the **Help** section in navigation

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Ctrl/Cmd + K` | Open quick search |
| `Ctrl/Cmd + N` | New message |
| `Esc` | Close modal/menu |

### Contact Support

If you need assistance:

1. Click **Help** in the navigation
2. Select **Contact Support**
3. Describe your issue
4. Submit the form
