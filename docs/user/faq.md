# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Verso?

Verso is a sovereign business operating system—a self-hosted platform that provides CRM, e-commerce, scheduling, content management, and internal communications all in one place. Unlike SaaS solutions, you own and control all your data.

### What makes Verso different from other platforms?

- **Data Sovereignty**: Your data stays on your servers
- **No Per-Seat Pricing**: Unlimited users for a fixed infrastructure cost
- **All-in-One**: No need to integrate multiple services
- **Open Source**: Full access to source code

### What are the system requirements?

- Python 3.10+
- PostgreSQL (or SQLite for small deployments)
- 2GB RAM minimum (4GB+ recommended)
- Any Linux VPS or dedicated server

## Account & Login

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Click the link and set a new password

### Why can't I log in?

Common causes:
- **Wrong email/password**: Double-check your credentials
- **Account deactivated**: Contact your administrator
- **Caps lock on**: Passwords are case-sensitive
- **Browser cache**: Try clearing cookies or incognito mode

### How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

1. Go to **Profile → Security**
2. Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
5. Save your backup codes somewhere safe

### What if I lose access to my 2FA device?

Use one of your backup codes to log in, then set up 2FA again with your new device. If you've lost your backup codes, contact your administrator for account recovery.

## E-commerce

### How do I add a product to my cart?

1. Browse the shop or search for a product
2. Click on the product to view details
3. Select any options (size, color, etc.)
4. Click **Add to Cart**
5. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

### What payment methods are accepted?

Verso supports credit/debit cards through Stripe:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Apple Pay and Google Pay (where supported)

### How do I track my order?

1. Go to **My Account → Order History**
2. Click on your order number
3. View current status and tracking information

### Can I cancel my order?

Contact support as soon as possible. Orders can typically be cancelled before they ship. Once shipped, you may need to initiate a return instead.

### How do refunds work?

Refunds are processed back to your original payment method. Allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

### How do I use a discount code?

1. Add items to your cart
2. On the cart page, find the **Discount Code** field
3. Enter your code and click **Apply**
4. The discount will be reflected in your total

### How do I check my gift card balance?

1. Go to **Shop → Gift Cards → Check Balance**
2. Enter your gift card code
3. View remaining balance

## Appointments & Scheduling

### How do I book an appointment?

1. Navigate to the booking page
2. Select a service type
3. Choose an available date and time
4. Enter your contact information
5. Confirm your booking

### Can I reschedule an appointment?

1. Go to **My Account → My Appointments**
2. Find the appointment you want to change
3. Click **Reschedule**
4. Select a new date and time
5. Confirm the change

### How do I cancel an appointment?

1. Go to **My Account → My Appointments**
2. Find the appointment
3. Click **Cancel Appointment**
4. Provide a reason (optional)
5. Confirm cancellation

Note: Cancellation policies may apply. Check for any fees before cancelling.

### What if I'm running late?

Contact the business directly by phone or use the messaging system to let them know. Most businesses appreciate advance notice.

## Messaging

### How do I send a message?

1. Click **Messages** in the navigation
2. Select a channel or start a new direct message
3. Type your message in the input field
4. Press Enter or click Send

### How do I create a new channel?

If you have permission:
1. Click the **+** icon next to Channels
2. Enter a channel name and description
3. Choose if it's public or private
4. Add members
5. Create the channel

### How do I mention someone?

Type `@` followed by their username. A dropdown will appear with matching users. Select the person you want to mention.

### Can I edit or delete messages?

- **Edit**: Click the message menu (⋮) and select Edit
- **Delete**: Click the message menu and select Delete

Note: Edited messages show an "edited" indicator.

### How do I search for messages?

Use the search bar at the top of the messages page. You can search:
- By keyword
- By sender (from:username)
- By channel (in:channel-name)

## Content & Blog

### How do I create a blog post?

If you have blogger permissions:
1. Go to **Content → Blog → New Post**
2. Add a title and content
3. Upload a featured image (optional)
4. Set the status to Published or save as Draft
5. Click Save

### Can I schedule posts for later?

Yes! When creating a post:
1. Set the status to **Scheduled**
2. Choose the publication date and time
3. Save the post

The post will automatically publish at the scheduled time.

### How do I add images to posts?

In the content editor:
1. Click the image icon
2. Upload a new image or select from media library
3. Add alt text for accessibility
4. Insert the image

## Technical Issues

### The page isn't loading properly

Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl/Cmd + R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Check your internet connection

### I'm seeing an error message

1. Note the error message and any error codes
2. Try refreshing the page
3. If it persists, contact support with:
   - Screenshot of the error
   - What you were trying to do
   - Time the error occurred

### The site is slow

Possible causes:
- High traffic periods
- Large file uploads
- Network issues
- Check your internet speed at fast.com

### I can't upload files

Check that:
- File size is under the limit (usually 10MB)
- File type is allowed (images, PDFs, documents)
- You have sufficient permissions

## Privacy & Data

### How is my data protected?

Verso uses:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- Password hashing
- Session security
- Regular security updates

### Can I download my data?

Yes! Go to **Profile → Privacy → Download My Data** to export your personal information.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to **Profile → Privacy**
2. Click **Request Account Deletion**
3. Confirm your request

Note: This is permanent and cannot be undone. Outstanding orders or appointments may need to be resolved first.

### Who can see my information?

- **Public**: Name on public posts/comments
- **Team**: Colleagues in your organization
- **Administrators**: Have access for support purposes
- **No one else**: Your data stays within the system

## Mobile Access

### Is there a mobile app?

Verso is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your phone:

**iOS (Safari):**
1. Open Verso in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Select **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap Add

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open Verso in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (⋮)
3. Select **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap Add

### Does Verso work offline?

Basic features work offline:
- View cached pages
- Draft messages (sent when online)
- Access help documentation

Full functionality requires an internet connection.
